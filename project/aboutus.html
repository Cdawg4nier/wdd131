Meet our Founder: Christian Fournier

I started this company because have always wanted to make games. Call of Duty Zombies, Pokemon, League of Legends, and Monster Hunter are just a few of the games that started my journey. As I've gotten older, I've fallen in love with smaller indie studios and games like Balatro, Outer Wilds, and Risk of Rain 2. These games were made by small, even 1 man teams, and it helped me realize I could turn my 6 year old self's dream of being a "game tester" into reality. As my first card game, Gode: Rogue, is nearing final production, I decided to make it official and start the game company I crafted years ago stocking shelves at a grocery store: Rexagon Labs.
